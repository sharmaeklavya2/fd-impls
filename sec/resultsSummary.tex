\section{Summary of Results}
\label{sec:summary}

We summarize implications among fairness notions in \cref{table:impls1},
and defer the proofs to \cref{sec:impls-extra}.
We state several non-implications in \cref{table:cex-add},
and defer the proofs to \cref{sec:cex-add-extra}.

In \cref{sec:impls-extra:tribool}, we prove additional implications when
marginals are $\{0, 1\}$ or $\{-1, 0\}$.
In \cref{sec:impls-extra:unit-demand}, we prove additional implications for unit-demand valuations.
In \cref{sec:cex-nonadd-extra}, we prove several non-implications for non-additive valuations.
We list results regarding the feasibility and infeasibility of fairness notions
in \cref{table:feas,table:infeas}, respectively, in \cref{sec:feas}.

We emphasize that these results were proved manually, i.e., without using the inference engine.
(The inference engine's results are stated implicitly in \cref{fig:additive-nny,sec:dags}.)
